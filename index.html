Convert PNG to WebP – Save Space and Boost Speed

In today’s fast-paced digital world, website speed plays a crucial role in user experience and SEO rankings. One effective way to enhance your site performance is by optimizing images. Converting PNG files to WebP format can reduce file sizes significantly without sacrificing quality.

Why Use WebP Format?

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. It enables faster loading times and better overall performance, especially on mobile devices.
